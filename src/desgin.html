Pine Hills Country Club

The Pavilion is a warm and rustic inspired clubhouse nestled in the rolling hills of the Pinehills community of Plymouth, Ma. It offers a unique setting with its airy vaulted ceilings, exposed wood beams, and wall of windows overlooking the beautifully manicured golf course.
